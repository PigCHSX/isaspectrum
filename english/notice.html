Station Announcements

  • Update Notice - Reply Function

    ISA Spectrum officially added a comment reply feature on May 27, 2026. To use it, first find the comment you want to reply to among everyone's comments, click the reply button, fill in the relevant information and reply content, then click publish. After passing the review, it will be posted. When a comment receives a reply, a notification will be sent to the contact email provided during commenting. For replies to others' comments, there will be a line "↳ Replying to @xxx (commenter's nickname)" below the nickname, and clicking it will jump to the original comment being replied to.

  • About The Anonymous

    The anonymous utilizes Supabase bucket technology to enable image uploading and display. Both desktop and WeChat platforms support uploading multiple images (on Windows press Ctrl/Mac press Cmd to multi-select, with a maximum of five images per comment and each image not exceeding 5MB). After submission, moderators will be notified for review. Approved comments will be publicly displayed (no email notification), while rejected ones will have the reason sent to the provided email. Moderators may not be the authors; if you believe it was mistakenly rejected, click the "Request Re-review" button in the email to contact the author. Additionally, starting today (May 27, 2026), all comments must include a contact email (used solely for notifications and no other purposes—please provide a valid, regularly checked email). Otherwise, notifications regarding rejected comments, deletions, or new replies will not be delivered. If no email is provided, ISA Spectrum will reject the comment to ensure functional integrity and timely community interaction, and the comment will not appear on the anonymous.

  • ISA Spectrum's Open Beta Notice

    ISA Spectrum began public beta testing on May 23, 2026, deployed via Cloudflare Pages using the default URL. A formal domain will be introduced later. If you'd like to join us, encounter issues, or have development suggestions, feel free to contact us (click "Contact Us" in the navigation bar).
